Our Goals

"If I had nine hours to chop down a tree, I'd spend the first six sharpening my axe."
-Abe Lincoln

Spending time preparing today can make a remarkable difference tomorrow. We believe that a promising future begins with planning, and that planning starts with questions. Our goal is to facilitate conversations about studentsâ€™ personal interests and academic pursuits to prepare for stable careers in the world of engineering. We wish to engage and encourage students in a time of academic and professional uncertainty, and strive to answer the questions that we ourselves were asking early in our scholastic journeys:

"What am I learning now that might be important later?"

"How can I balance my social life with my academic schedule?"

"When might I be academically satisfied - is there an end to all of the studying?"

Classroom learning today is very different than it was a year ago. Though we at YEP! may never experience what a classroom is like in this new world, we hope to help guide early ambitions for academic and professional goals. We are a small house of locally grown Colorado engineers who wish to grow our network within our community by giving back - one question at a time.
