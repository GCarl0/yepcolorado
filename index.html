Our Goals

"If I had nine hours to chop down a tree, I'd spend the first six sharpening my axe."
-Abe Lincoln

Spending time preparing today can make a remarkable difference tomorrow. We believe that a promising future begins with planning, and that planning starts with questions. Our goal is to bridge the gap between college-bound students and early-career engineers by facilitating conversations about studentsâ€™ own personal interests within engineering. We wish to engage students in a time of academic and professional uncertainty, and strive to answer the questions that we ourselves were asking early in our scholastic journeys:

"What is it like to be a modern-day engineer?"

"When might I be academically satisfied - is a bachelor's degree enough?"

"What type(s) of engineering should I specialize in - which fields might interest me?"

Classroom learning today is very different than it was a year ago. Though many of us at YEP! may never understand what a classroom is like during these times, we hope to help guide early ambitions for academic and professional goals by sharing our experiences. We are a group of locally grown Colorado engineers who wish to branch out within our community by giving back - one question at a time.
